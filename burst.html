Slice of the Pie

The chart below represents all of the restaurants in Ann Arbor, broken up in various ways from the center moving outwards. The center circle represents the total number of restaurants for Ann Arbor. The ring closest to the center displays the types of cuisines, with longer rings indicating a greater quantity of restaurants in that cuisine category. The rating of that cuisine category, derived from an averaging the ratings of all restaurants belonging it, can be found in the cuisine tag in the right upon hover. At the next level out, each cuisine is broken up by price, from low ($) to high ($$$$). The outermost ring is comprised of individual restaurants within that cuisine and price range. The length (radius) of the restaurant segment indicates the rating, with higher ratings corresponding to greater lengths. To assist with visual comparison, "rating rings" have been provided (one for each number between 1 and 5). The opacity of the restaurant segment reflects the number of reviews that have contributed to that rating (fewer reviews is reflected by a more faded color). Hover over, or click on, a section to see more detail and learn more about it. To zoom out, click the center of the circle.
